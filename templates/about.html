About Plants Paradise

Bringing nature to your home, one plant at a time

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Plants Paradise began with a simple mission: to make the joy of plant parenthood accessible to everyone.

What started as a small local nursery has grown into a beloved destination for plant enthusiasts across the country. Our founder, Sarah Green, discovered her passion for plants during a difficult time in her life, and she wanted to share that healing power with others.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers, helping them create their own little pieces of paradise at home. Every plant we sell is carefully selected and nurtured with love, ensuring you receive healthy, thriving plants that will bring life and beauty to your space.
